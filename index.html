About Bag of Cakes

Where Artistry Meets
Deliciousness in Irvine

Bag of Cakes was born from a simple belief: every celebration deserves a cake that tastes as extraordinary as it looks. Our founder, a classically trained pastry chef, brought European techniques and a passion for creative design to Irvine, creating a bakery that truly stands apart.

Every morning, our kitchen fills with the aroma of fresh butter, melting chocolate, and vanilla bean. Each cake, pastry, and treat is made from scratch using premium ingredients. We never take shortcuts because we believe the difference is in the details.

From intimate birthday celebrations to grand wedding receptions, we approach every order with the same dedication and creativity. Your cake is not just a dessert. It is the centerpiece of your celebration, and we treat it that way.
